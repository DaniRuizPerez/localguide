# Privacy Policy — AI Offline Tour Guide

**Last updated:** 2026-05-07

AI Offline Tour Guide ("the app") is published by Daniel Ruiz Perez.

## What we collect
Nothing. The app does not collect, store on a server, or share any personal data. There is no account, no login, no analytics SDK.

## What stays on your device
- Your **location** is used on the device to find nearby places via Wikipedia's public geosearch and to draw the map.
- Your **voice** is transcribed on the device by Android's speech recognition.
- **Photos** taken with the camera button are processed on the device by the on-device AI model.

None of this leaves your phone.

## What we connect to
- **Wikipedia** (en.wikipedia.org) for place information.
- **OpenStreetMap routing** (router.project-osrm.org) for walking distances.
- **Google Maps SDK** for the map tiles.
- **Hugging Face** (huggingface.co) **once**, to download the AI model (~557 MB).

No advertising. No tracking. No third-party analytics.

## Permissions
- **Location:** to find places near you.
- **Microphone:** to convert your speech to text (on-device).
- **Camera:** to let you ask questions about what you see (on-device).
- **Internet:** to download the AI model and reach the services listed above.

## Children's privacy
The app is rated for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
